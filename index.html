Inside Guwa

The activity stays clear. Everything else stays quiet.

Today’s Quest is ready when you open the app. The catalog, Trail, badges, and custom activities are close by without turning your day into another dashboard.

Your personal trail

Small days become something you can look back on.

Completed activities appear in your calendar and timeline. Notes, moods, badges, streaks, goals, and local photo memories stay personal instead of becoming a public feed.

  • Photos remain on your device.
  • Google Sign-In is optional.
  • Account Backup syncs progress, not photo files.
