A gentle loop

Find something. Go do it. Keep the memory.

No pressure to keep up. Guwa gives you a small idea when you want one and a quiet place to remember it afterward.

  1. 01

    Find an activity

    Open Today’s Quest or browse the catalog by category, time, difficulty, and place.

  2. 02

    Go experience it

    Start the activity, reroll when it does not fit, or lock it for later.

  3. 03

    Keep the memory

    Complete it with an optional note and mood. Guwa Pro can add a local photo memory.

Explore activities

A catalog for the kind of day you actually have.

Browse cafe, creative, culture, explore, nature, reflection, and treat ideas. Longer monthly activities appear as a distinct Big Quest when enabled.

Keep your memories

Small days become something you can look back on.

Completed activities appear in your calendar and timeline. Notes, moods, and local photo memories stay personal instead of becoming a public feed.

  • Photo files remain on your device.
  • Google Sign-In is optional.
  • Account Backup syncs progress, not photo files.

Progress your way

A trail, not a leaderboard.

Badges, streaks, goals, and statistics reflect what you have done without turning ordinary life into a competition.

Guwa Pro

More room when you want it.

Guwa stays useful for free. Pro removes catalog and custom-activity limits and unlocks local photo memories, richer recap exports, and eligible Account Backup.

  • Unlimited catalog startsNo weekly wait between catalog choices.
  • Unlimited custom activitiesCreate and save more plans of your own.
  • Photo memoriesAdd camera or gallery moments that stay local.
  • Account BackupOptional progress backup after Google Sign-In.
