How it works

Small quests, simple memories.

Questle is built for casual solo exploration. No pressure, no social feed, no complicated setup.

1

Discover

Roll a solo activity idea for your day, from quick cozy tasks to small outdoor adventures.

2

Complete

Finish the quest, write a note or mood, and build a trail of completed experiences.

3

Remember

Review your calendar, badges, and memories over time. Photo memories stay on your device.

Privacy-first by design. Questle works without an account. Google sign-in is optional and used for progress backup features. Photo memories are local-only and are not uploaded to Questle cloud backup.
