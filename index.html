How it works

Small enough to do today. Meaningful enough to remember.

Questle is built for casual solo exploration: simple prompts, quick completion, and a private record of small experiences that make the week feel more intentional.

1

Roll a Questle

Get a solo activity suggestion based on practical everyday moments.

2

Complete it your way

Finish the quest, add an optional note or mood, and attach a local photo memory when you want to remember it.

3

Build your trail

Review your calendar, streaks, badges, and saved memories without a public feed or leaderboard.

For everyday life

Create your own Questles

Save personal ideas like a bookstore visit, a solo dinner, a park walk, a photo challenge, or a small reset ritual.

Progress without pressure

Streaks, goals, and badges

Questle tracks your completed moments over time with simple progress, not public ranking.

Privacy-first by design. Questle works as a local-first app. Your quest history, notes, moods, streaks, badges, custom Questles, and photo memories are stored on your device by default. For policy details, read the Privacy Policy or contact us through Support.
