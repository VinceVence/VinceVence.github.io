Questle

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Questle is a lifestyle app for discovering solo activities, completing quests, saving memories, and tracking personal progress.

Information Questle stores on your device

Questle stores app activity locally on your device, including onboarding state, completed quests, streaks, badges, notes, moods, calendar history, and optional photos you choose to attach to quest memories.

Photos and camera access

Questle may ask for access to your camera or photo library only when you choose to add a photo to a quest memory. Photos are used for the memory feature and are stored locally on your device. Questle does not upload your photos to a Questle server.

Purchases

Questle may offer optional Pro features through Google Play Billing. Purchase status may be processed through RevenueCat so the app can verify whether Pro features are unlocked. Payment processing is handled by Google Play.

Feedback form

Questle may link to an external feedback form for bug reports, suggestions, and support requests. Information you submit through that form is provided voluntarily and may include your message and any contact information you choose to provide.

Data sharing

Questle does not sell your personal data. Questle may use service providers such as Google Play and RevenueCat only as needed to support app distribution, purchases, entitlement verification, and app functionality.

Data retention and deletion

Quest history, notes, moods, badges, streaks, and optional photos are stored locally on your device. You can remove locally stored app data by deleting the app or clearing the app's storage through your device settings. Feedback form submissions may be retained as needed to respond to issues or improve the app.

Children

Questle is not directed to children. The app is intended for a general audience.

Health, medical, and financial disclaimer

Questle is not a medical, therapy, diagnosis, treatment, financial, banking, investment, or emergency support app. Mood notes and reflections are optional personal journaling features only.
