Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Questle helps users discover solo activities, complete quests, save memories, and track progress.

Local app data

Questle stores quest progress, notes, moods, badges, streaks, custom quests, and optional photo memories on your device.

Photo memories

Photo memories stay on your device. Questle does not upload photo files to cloud backup.

Google sign-in and backup

Questle can be used without an account. Optional Google sign-in uses Firebase Authentication. Optional progress backup may store progress data in Firebase Firestore for signed-in users.

Purchases

Questle may offer Pro features through Google Play Billing. RevenueCat may be used to verify Pro entitlement status.

Use the support page to contact Questle.
