Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Questle helps users discover solo activities, complete quests, save memories, and track progress.

Local app data

Questle stores quest progress, notes, moods, badges, streaks, calendar history, custom quests, and optional photo memories on your device.

Photo memories

Photo memories stay on your device. Questle does not upload photo files, image bytes, local photo paths, camera metadata, or gallery metadata to cloud backup.

Google sign-in and backup

Questle can be used without an account. Optional Google sign-in uses Firebase Authentication. Optional progress backup may store quest progress data in Firebase Firestore for signed-in users.

Purchases

Questle may offer Pro features through Google Play Billing. RevenueCat may be used to verify Pro entitlement status. Payment processing is handled by Google Play.

Support and feedback

Support messages are voluntary and may include the information you choose to send, such as your message, app version, device details, or contact information.

Service providers

Questle does not sell personal data. Questle may use Firebase, Google Play, and RevenueCat to support authentication, app distribution, purchase verification, progress backup, and app functionality.

Retention and removal

Local Questle data stays on your device until you remove app data from the device. Cloud progress backup data for a signed-in account can be removed by following the account deletion instructions.

Account deletion instructions

Important disclaimer

Questle is not a medical, therapy, financial, banking, investment, or emergency support app. Mood notes and reflections are optional personal journaling features only.
