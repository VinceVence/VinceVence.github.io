Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Questle helps users discover solo activities, complete quests, save memories, and track progress.

Local app data

Questle stores quest progress, notes, moods, badges, streaks, calendar history, custom quests, and optional photo memories on your device by default.

Photo memories

Photo memories stay on your device. Questle does not upload photo files, image bytes, local photo paths, camera metadata, or gallery metadata.

Purchases

Questle may offer paid features through Google Play Billing. Payment processing is handled by Google Play.

Support and feedback

Support messages are voluntary and may include the information you choose to send, such as your message, app version, device details, or contact information.

Service providers

Questle does not sell personal data. Questle may use Google Play to support app distribution, purchases, and app functionality.

Retention and removal

Local Questle data stays on your device until you remove app data from the device or uninstall Questle. Support messages are retained only as needed to respond to the request.

Account deletion information

Important disclaimer

Questle is not a medical, therapy, financial, banking, investment, or emergency support app. Mood notes and reflections are optional personal journaling features only.
