Account deletion

Questle is local-first and does not currently create a public profile for your quests. Your quest history, notes, moods, streaks, custom Questles, and photo memories stay on your device by default.

Remove local app data

You can remove Questle data from your device by clearing the app storage in Android settings or uninstalling Questle.
