Account deletion

Questle can be used without an account. If you used Google sign-in or progress backup, you can request removal of cloud account data associated with Questle.

How to request removal

Email vencer.lanzvincent09@gmail.com with the subject line: Questle Account Deletion.

Include the Google account email you used to sign in to Questle so the request can be matched to the correct Firebase user record.

What will be removed

  • Firebase Authentication account data associated with Questle, where applicable
  • Cloud progress backup data stored for that Questle account
  • Support request records tied to the request, where practical

What stays on your device

Photo memories and local Questle app data are stored on your device. You can remove local data by clearing the app storage or uninstalling Questle from the device.

Timing

Requests are handled as soon as practical. You may receive a follow-up email if more information is needed to verify the correct account.
